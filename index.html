Rules

How the pool works.

Group stage: read the room, not just the scores

Predict the full 1st-to-4th order in every group. Exact placements are worth 4, near misses are worth 1, the correct top two teams add 2, and a perfect group adds 4. That means each group is worth up to 22 points.

  • Exact position: 4 points
  • Off by 1 place: 1 point
  • Correct top 2 teams: +2 points
  • Perfect group order: +4 points

Tranches: Perfect = 22, Excellent = 17–21, Good = 12–16.

Knockout stage: where bold calls pay

The knockout competition is separate from the group stage. Correct advance picks earn more or fewer points based on the odds at the time, so long-shot picks are rewarded without making the pool feel random.

  • Group stage and knockout stage are separate competitions
  • Each competition gets 50% of the total pot
  • Longer odds = bigger points
  • Score each correct advance pick on its own merit
